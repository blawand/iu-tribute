LILAC (라일락)

5th Studio Album | Released: March 25, 2021

Celebrating the end of her twenties and greeting her thirties, 'LILAC' offers a vibrant farewell to a chapter of youth. The album features a disco-infused pop sound, reflecting on memories with a bright and festive energy, signifying a glamorous and hopeful transition.
