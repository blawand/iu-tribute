Palette (팔레트)

4th Studio Album | Released: April 21, 2017

Marking her 25th year, 'Palette' delves into self-discovery, growth, and embracing one's own unique colors. It blends pop sensibilities with introspective lyrics and sophisticated production, featuring collaborations with artists like G-Dragon and Oh Hyuk.

Music Video Concept: Palette (Feat. G-DRAGON)

The 'Palette' MV is a visual feast exploring IU's personal tastes and transition into adulthood (age 25). It contrasts youthful elements with more mature aesthetics, referencing pop culture ('Leon', 'Twitty Bird') and art history. The changing color palettes symbolize her evolving identity and acceptance of her multifaceted self. G-Dragon's appearance adds a layer of experienced advice.

Live Performance Highlights: Dear Name (이름에게)

"Dear Name" is often cited as one of IU's most vocally and emotionally demanding songs, dedicated to comforting those who feel lost or unseen. Key performances include:

  • Melon Music Awards (MMA) 2017: A legendary performance featuring aspiring singers, creating a powerful, tear-jerking stage that resonated deeply with audiences.
  • Yoo Huiyeol's Sketchbook: Raw, intimate performances showcasing her incredible vocal control and emotional delivery in a simpler setting.
  • Concert Performances ('dlwlrma', 'Love, Poem'): Often a show-stopping finale or encore, delivered with immense passion and connection to the audience.
