My Mister (나의 아저씨)

Drama | tvN | 2018 | 16 Episodes

Director: Kim Won-seok | Writer: Park Hae-young

Synopsis

"My Mister" tells the story of three middle-aged brothers enduring the weight of their lives and a cold, resilient young woman, Lee Ji-an, who has weathered harsh realities. They come together, learn from each other's struggles, and ultimately find warmth and healing in their unlikely connection, navigating the bleakness of urban life.

Character: Lee Ji-an

IU delivered a career-defining performance as Lee Ji-an, a young woman burdened by debt and trauma, forced into a life of stoicism and distrust. Ji-an is resourceful, observant, and deeply wounded, keeping the world at arm's length. IU masterfully portrayed Ji-an's subtle shifts from numb survival to tentative vulnerability, showcasing profound emotional depth with minimal dialogue, relying heavily on her expressive eyes and nuanced body language. It was a transformative role that earned widespread critical acclaim.

Reception

"My Mister" received universal acclaim from critics and audiences alike for its realistic portrayal of life's struggles, masterful writing, directing, and exceptional performances. Lee Ji-eun's portrayal of Lee Ji-an was particularly lauded as a raw, powerful, and unforgettable performance, shattering idol actress stereotypes and cementing her status as a serious actress. The drama won numerous awards, including Best Drama at the Baeksang Arts Awards.
