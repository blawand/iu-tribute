A Legacy of Recognition

Celebrating IU's numerous awards and achievements.

Major Awards (Daesang - Grand Prize)

IU's consistent excellence has been recognized with numerous Daesangs (Grand Prizes), the highest honor at South Korean music awards shows.

  • Melon Music Awards (MMA): Artist of the Year (2014, 2017, 2021), Album of the Year (2011 - Real+, 2017 - Palette, 2021 - LILAC), Song of the Year (2011 - Good Day)
  • Golden Disc Awards (GDA): Digital Daesang (Song of the Year) (2018 - Through the Night, 2021 - Blueming, 2022 - Celebrity, 2024 - Love wins all [Placeholder]), Disc Daesang (Album of the Year) (2022 - LILAC)
  • Korean Music Awards (KMA): Song of the Year (2012 - Good Day), Musician of the Year (2024 [Placeholder])
